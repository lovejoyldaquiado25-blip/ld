Logo & Mark

The Mark

Wordmark · Symbol

The mark reads as a stylized "U" and ascending line — an arrow forever climbing, never plateauing. It's built to work in one weight only: a champagne-to-forest gradient on dark, and a solid forest mark on light, so it stays legible everywhere from a favicon to a jersey chest print.

Palette Logic

A near-black forest green anchors every screen — it reads as focused and calm rather than corporate. Teal and cyan carry all interactive energy: links, CTAs, highlights. Cream and sage close the loop as a soft "champagne" gradient reserved for moments of arrival — hero type, section closers, the logo itself.
