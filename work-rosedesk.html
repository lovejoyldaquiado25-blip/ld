Identity Direction

The Rose Desk needed to feel personal and approachable — not another corporate agency deck. A blush-and-crimson palette paired with a serif display face keeps the brand warm, while every CTA stays high-contrast crimson so the site never loses its selling edge.

Palette Logic

Crimson pink carries every call-to-action and highlight; nothing else on the page competes with it. Blush and petal-white keep backgrounds soft and personal, while near-black ink handles all reading copy for maximum contrast.
